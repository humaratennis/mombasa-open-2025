Tournament Rules & Information

Welcome to the Mombasa Open! Please review the rules below for a smooth and fair competition. All pool matches must be completed by 2:00 PM Sunday.

🎾 Humara Tennis Mombasa Open 2025 – Terms & Conditions

  1. Eligibility
    • Players must be 12 years and above on the day of the tournament.
    • All minors must be accompanied by a parent or legal guardian.
    • By registering, participants confirm they are physically fit and capable of competing.
  2. Registration & Fees
    • Entry fee is KES 1,000 per category.
    • No entries will be accepted on the day of the tournament.
    • Entry fees are non-refundable under any circumstances.
  3. Tournament Format
    • The tournament follows a Pool Round-Robin stage followed by Knockout rounds.
    • Match format: First to 4 games, win by 2. At 4–4, a 7-point tiebreak is played (win by 2).
    • Organizers reserve the right to adjust the format depending on entries or conditions.
  4. Seeding & Draws
    • Seeding will be based on past Humara Tennis Tournament results.
    • Only the draw posted online will be considered official.
  5. Code of Conduct
    • Players must follow the rules and spirit of tennis at all times.
    • Unsportsmanlike behavior, verbal abuse, or equipment damage may lead to disqualification.
    • Proper tennis attire is required on court.
  6. Punctuality
    • Players must report at least 30 minutes before their scheduled match time.
    • A no-show after 15 minutes of match call time will result in a walkover to the opponent.
  7. Injury & Medical
    • All players participate at their own risk.
    • The organizers are not liable for any injuries sustained during the tournament.
    • Basic first aid may be available at the venue.
  8. Weather Policy
    • Matches may be postponed or rescheduled in case of bad weather.
    • No refunds will be issued due to weather-related disruptions.
  9. Prizes
    • Prizes will be awarded from the quarterfinal stage to the final winners.
    • Prize structure is determined by the tournament committee and is non-transferable.
  10. Media & Consent
    • By registering, participants and/or guardians agree to the use of photos or videos taken during the event for promotional purposes.
    • Names and match results may be published online or in print.
  11. Disputes
    • Any protest or dispute must be reported immediately to the tournament director.
    • The tournament director’s decision will be final and binding.
  12. Club Rules
    • All players and guests must respect and follow the hosting club’s rules and regulations at all times.
